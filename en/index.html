Study & Research Interests

  • Foundations of physics, nonequilibrium statistical physics, condensed-matter theory, strongly correlated quantum many-body theory, and numerical simulations
  • First to unveil the nonequilibrium criticality of fermions (critical relaxation and driven dynamics), discovering finite-time scaling beyond the Kibble-Zurek mechanism
  • Proposed a theoretical framework that overcomes fermion sign problems through nonequilibrium methods
  • Currently focuses on nonequilibrium critical dynamics with emergent supersymmetry and on sign problems in frustrated bosonic/spin systems
